About Me

Rajarshi Chattopadhyay is a System Engineer at IBM - Client Innovation Centre, India, where he is currently serving as Application Developer in Distribution sector - Shipping domain.
He currently works in the areas of Software Development, Data mining and Analysis. He was intern at Bharat Sanchar Nigam Ltd. and SIEMENS Metal technologies, where he worked on Computer Networking and Automation respectively. He also worked part-time at Hi-Q Solutions on embedded systems.

Rajarshi received his Bachelor of Technology degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering from West Bengal University of Technology (WBUT), Kolkata, India.

His cross disciplinary research interests lie in areas of Big Data mining and analysis, Applied Machine Learning, and Artificial Intelligence in Embedded Systems and Internet-of-Things.
